

# CloudSync - ICT Solutions Website

A modern, professional landing page for **CloudSync**, an ICT services company. The website will be a single-page marketing site built from top to bottom matching the provided design references.

## Sections (in order)

### 1. Top Banner & Navigation
- Blue announcement bar with company tagline
- Sticky header with CloudSync logo, nav links (Home, About, Services, Blog, Contact), and a "Consult" CTA button
- Scrolling marquee ticker with service highlights

### 2. Hero Section
- Large headline: "Transform **Your Business** Through **Strategic** IT Solutions"
- Hero image of person with VR headset on pink background
- Trustpilot badge (4.9 rating)

### 3. Client Logos Bar
- Logo marquee featuring Netflix, Framer, Reddit, Tesla, Uber branding

### 4. About Us Section
- Stats card (88% revenue growth, 99.8% client satisfaction)
- Image of team member
- "Comprehensive IT Solution Growth & Efficiency" heading
- 78% circular progress chart with service tags (Cybersecurity, Infrastructure Management, Operational Excellence)
- Three value propositions: Innovation at our core, Simplifying complexity, Empowering growth

### 5. Working Roadmap Section
- Dark navy background
- 8-step process displayed as colorful circles: Brainstorming → UX Research → Product Designing → Front-End Development → Usability Testing → Back-End Development → SEO Optimization → Digital Marketing
- "2.3M+" stat and "Get Started" CTA

### 6. Global Growth Stats Section
- Headline: "We Makes Globally **Growth & Increase** Revenue"
- Six stat cards with metrics ($29k, 88%, 3x, etc.) in blue rounded shapes

### 7. Services Section ("What We Offering")
- Headline: "Provides Full-Cycle Custom IT Development Services"
- Description paragraph
- Three service cards: IT Management Service, Website Design & Development, ICT Consultation

### 8. Tech Solution / CTA Section
- "The Complete Platform **To Power Your** Operations"
- Description text with "Contact Today!" button
- Team collaboration image

### 9. Why Choose Us Section
- Team photo with floating design tool icons (Figma)
- Headline about expertise, innovation, and client-first approach
- Feature cards: Experienced Professionals, Client-Centric Approach

### 10. Portfolio / Work Showcase Section
- Dark background with "Projects That Speak for Us" heading
- Horizontal carousel of project screenshots (Food Recipes App, Mobile Application, Blockchain Application, Online Store)

### 11. Footer
- Dark background with CloudSync logo and description
- Three columns: The Company (links), Service (links), Locations & Contact info
- Copyright bar with social media icons (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram)
- Scroll-to-top button

## Design Style
- **Primary color**: Deep navy blue (#1a1f4e) and bright blue (#3b5bff)
- **Accent colors**: Pink/magenta for highlights, orange/yellow for accents
- **Typography**: Clean, modern — mix of serif for headlines and sans-serif for body
- **Overall feel**: Professional, tech-forward, clean with rounded shapes and bold stats
- **Responsive**: Fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile
